About Us

At SRKR Engineering College, we are committed to fostering academic excellence and innovation.our institution stands as a beacon of advanced learning and research in engineering and technology.

Established with a vision to create leaders and innovators, we offer a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in their careers. Our state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and vibrant campus life create an environment that encourages intellectual curiosity and personal growth. Join us at SRKR Engineering College, where tradition meets technology and education transforms into excellence.
