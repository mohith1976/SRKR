Technology Centre - IHUB - AICTEIDEALAB Facilities

1. Technology Centre - Skill Improvement Programs / Workshops
2. Digital Learning Centre - MOOCs Courses
3. Innovation Centre - Design Thinking & Ideation Programs
4. Incubation Centre - I-Hub, MSME & Startups
5. AICTE IDEALAB - Ideation, Prototype, Product, Commercialization & Consultancy

Technology Centre has been established with an aim to facilitate technology training and various skill development programs / workshops for all the students of SRKR Engineering College. The centre concentrates on skill development in latest technologies and encourages the students to do projects in all disciplines of engineering / technology. Technology Centre has been well equipped with a dedicated computer lab with all necessary facilities.

Technology Centre objectives are :

  • To develop and implement support strategies and methodologies of effective use of information technology.
  • To maintain reliable technological infrastructures, and provide continuous improvement and technical support to the students and staff.
  • To plan, organize and provide training that will enhance students' knowledge and skills that are increasingly fundamental for students in educational institutions.
  • To organize courses to increase information technology literacy, exposure, and skills of SRKREC students that will be essential in the students' job career.

I-Hub (Innovation, Incubation, Industry)

I-Hub has been established with an aim to promote innovation and entrepreneurship among students and teaching staff of SRKR Engineering College. The centre concentrates on latest technologies, new idea generation and encourages the students to do projects, prototypes, and ideations in all disciplines of engineering. The SRKR i-Hub nurtures innovation, and encourages entrepreneurial skills.

AP CM Skill Excellence Centre

About APSSDC

APSSDC was formed to bring faster execution of skill initiatives, coordinate and synergize skilling efforts of all departments. It acts as a nodal agency for promotion of skill development, entrepreneurship, and innovation in the State. APSSDC covers all groups, both academic and non-academic. One major group under academic is the engineering profession.

About CM Skill Excellence Centers (CMSEC)

In order to scale up and increase the number of skilled candidates in Engineering Colleges and improve placement opportunities, APSSDC is planning to set up two labs under CMSEC:

1. Skill Excellence Centers - Computing Labs

Knowledge Partners: Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Udacity, Coursera, etc.

2. Skill Excellence Center - Design, Automation, and Simulation Labs with AR/VR

Knowledge Partner: Dassault Systèmes
